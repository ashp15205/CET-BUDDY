🎓 What is CETBuddy?

CETBuddy is your assistant for exploring B.E./B.Tech colleges in Maharashtra using your MHT-CET percentile. Just enter your percentile and filter by category, branch, or college to get a customized list based on official CAP Round 1 cutoff data.

🙌 Why CETBuddy?

  • Quick & Easy: Instantly find eligible engineering colleges based on your percentile.
  • Zero Signup: No login, no ads, no data tracking — 100% free to use.
  • PDF Export: Download your personalized colleges list with a single click.

📝 What You Need

  1. Your MHT-CET Percentile (e.g. 96.45)
  2. Your Category (e.g. GOPENS, LOPENH)
  3. Preferred College or Branch (optional)
