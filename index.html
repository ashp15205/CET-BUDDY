🎓 What is CETBuddy?

CETBuddy is your personal tool to explore B.E./B.Tech colleges in Maharashtra using your MHT-CET score. Just enter your percentile and category to get a customized list based on official CAP Round 1 cutoff data.

🚀 How It Works

  • ✅ Uses official 2024-25 CAP Round 1 cutoffs
  • ✅ Filters by percentile, category, college, or branch
  • ✅ Generates an eligible college list instantly

📝 What You Need

  1. Your MHT-CET Percentile (e.g. 96.45)
  2. Your Category (e.g. GOPENS, LOPENH)
  3. Preferred College or Branch (optional)

🔔 Disclaimer: CETBuddy is for informational use only. Results are based on public CAP Round 1 MHT-CET data for 2024-25. Please verify with official sources like cetcell.mahacet.org.

Source: CAP Round 1 Cutoffs.
